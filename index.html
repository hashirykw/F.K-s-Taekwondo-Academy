The Academy

An elite Taekwondo family
in Karachi

F.K's Taekwondo & Fitness Academy teaches and promotes Taekwondo as a complete self-defence system — not just a sport. Training runs from the Liaquat Ali Khan Sports Complex in Gulberg Town under Head Coach Faizan Majeed, a 3rd Dan black belt certified in Korea.

Head Coach

Faizan Majeed

Black Belt, 3rd Dan — certified in Korea. Students consistently mention the same thing in reviews: the instructor actually pays attention to each student.

Boys and girls, side by side

The academy trains boys and girls from age four upward, with the five tenets of Taekwondo — courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control and indomitable spirit — built into every session.

Programs

Every class blends technique, conditioning and the discipline that makes Taekwondo more than exercise.

01

Taekwondo

The core syllabus — stances, kicks, forms and structured belt progression toward black belt.

  • Full belt system
  • Kids and adults
  • Beginners welcome
02

Self-Defence

Taekwondo taught the way it was intended — as a practical, ancient self-defence system.

  • Situation-based drills
  • Confidence building
  • Boys & girls
03

Poomsae

Forms training — precision, balance and control, and the route to every belt grading.

  • Grading preparation
  • Technique refinement
  • All belt levels
04

Sparring

Controlled contact rounds with proper gear, once the fundamentals are solid.

  • Protective equipment
  • Supervised rounds
  • Tournament prep
05

Fitness

Conditioning, flexibility and strength work that supports everything else on the mat.

  • Mobility & stretching
  • Strength circuits
  • All fitness levels
06

Little Tigers

A gentle introduction for the youngest students, starting from four years old.

  • Coordination games
  • Basic kicks & blocks
  • Short sessions
